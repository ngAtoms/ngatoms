Card

A frosted glass container for grouping related content and actions. Composed of header, content, and footer slots — use all three or just the ones you need.

Usage

Add the card to your project and import the directive set wherever you need it.

$ ngatoms add card

Import all six card directives in your component:

Anatomy

The card is composed of four optional directives: ngAtomsCardHeader, ngAtomsCardTitle, ngAtomsCardDescription, ngAtomsCardContent, and ngAtomsCardFooter. Use any combination.

Interactive

Add role="button" or the nga-card--interactive class when the entire card is the action target. The card lifts on hover.

Clickable card

Click anywhere on this card to trigger the action.

The entire surface is interactive.

With badge

Compose cards freely — add badges, code snippets, separators, or other primitives inside the content slots.
